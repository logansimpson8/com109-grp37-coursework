Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions answered in one place.

What services does the gym offer?

We provide strength training, cardio equipment, personal coaching, and group fitness classes designed for all experience levels.

Do I need a membership to visit?

Yes, a membership is required for full access. However, we also offer day passes for visitors who want to try the gym before joining.

How do I contact the gym?

You can use the contact form on our website. Once submitted, you’ll see a confirmation message letting you know it was received.

Can I freeze my gym membership?

Yes, we allow you to freeze you membership for a period of time anywhere from 3 weeks to 3 Months.

Is there a trial membership program?

Yes, we have a one-day trial membership which allows you to see all the benefits of joining our gym.

What are some essential etiquette and logistics?

Always wipe down equipment after use, return weights to racks, bring a padlock for lockers, and wear comfortable, breathable clothing with supportive shoes.

Where is the gym located?

You can find our exact location on the interactive map in the Contact section of the website.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we offer one‑on‑one personal training sessions tailored to your goals. Contact us for availability.

Can I cancel or change my membership?

Membership changes can be made by contacting our support team. We’ll help you adjust or cancel your plan anytime.
